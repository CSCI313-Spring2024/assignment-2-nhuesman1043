A Short Autobiography:

Throughout my childhood, I always had an increasing fascination in computers. When my freshman year of highschool rolled around, I had finally saved up enough money from working my Summer job at a greenhouse to buy all the parts needed for building my very own personal computer. It was then that I truly discovered my life's passion. After some time, I graduated high school and began working at Marvin Windows. This was also a critical point in my life as another interest of mine was uncovered. While I started as a Production Associate at Marvin, about a year later I moved into the position of Systems Automation Intern. This experience opened my eyes to a whole new world of possibilities, combining my technical skills with an understanding of production processes. Today, these two chapters of my life have converged in my career aspirations. I envision a future where I leverage this unique blend to create impactful production-facing web applications.
